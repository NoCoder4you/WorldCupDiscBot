  1. All participants (“Players”) must pay a one-time, non-recurring entry fee, set as a specified amount of Habbo credits, prior to the stated entry deadline.

  3. Entry fees are pooled to fund the prize pool and may be subject to reasonable tax, platform, or administrative deductions where applicable.
  4. No discounts, waivers, substitutions, or alternative forms of entry are permitted.
  5. Entry fees are strictly non-refundable once paid.
  6. Entry closes at 23:59 UTC on INPUT DATE. No new Players shall be accepted after the official kick-off.

2.2 Team Draw

  1. Each confirmed Player is randomly assigned one (1) national team by the bot following the close of the entry period.
  2. The assignment process is automated and impartial. No manual bias, weighting, preference, or selective treatment is applied.
  3. Final team assignment may be deferred until all forty-eight (48) national teams have been purchased by Players.
  4. Draw results may be published in a dedicated server channel for transparency and audit, including machine-readable files such as JSON.
  5. Publication of the draw constitutes official confirmation of assignment.
  6. Assignments are final upon publication and may not be swapped except via explicitly bot-sanctioned mechanisms defined elsewhere.
  7. All teams commence on equal terms provided all 48 teams are allocated before the opening match.

2.3 Team Reveal

  1. Assigned teams will be revealed via an official Referee announcement embed no later than one (1) week prior to kick-off.
  2. Nicknames and roles may be updated for visibility, subject to platform limits.
  3. DMs may be sent confirming assignments; failure to receive a DM does not invalidate assignment.
